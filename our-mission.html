Coding is about more than just computers — it's about problem solving.

This analytical way of thinking is at the core of all innovation in science and technology.

As Applied Physics PhD students at Harvard, we know firsthand that coding is a necessary skill across many fields. But above all, we believe computer science is empowering. It gives kids the ability to understand how the technology around them works and the tools to turn their curiosity into creation.

At WeeCode we teach kids to code because we believe children shouldn't just use technology — they should shape it.
