Applied Physics Ph.D. Candidate, Harvard University

After studying Physics at Stanford, Salma aims to share the same joy and wonder she felt learning to code as a young girl.

And while studying Physics at Boston University, Luke experienced first-hand the impact of quality teaching through his extensive experience tutoring fellow undergraduates.

Now, as Applied Physics PhD students at Harvard, Luke and Salma experience every day the challenging yet rewarding process of pursuing progress in science and technology. Their shared belief that an early introduction to STEM can change the course of a child's life inspired them to take action in their community.

Together, they founded Wee STEM to bring hands-on learning to young minds in the Boston area. Our introductory coding courses are designed to spark imagination, build strong foundations, and inspire kids to see themselves as creators of the future.
